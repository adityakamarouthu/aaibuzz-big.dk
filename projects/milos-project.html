Milos

DLRC, DUBAI

TYPOLOGY

Residential

SIZE M2/FT2

STATUS

DESIGN COMPLETED - TENDER STAGE

Milos sets a new benchmark for refined living in Dubai, strategically located in Dubai Land for effortless access to the city's iconic landmarks and premier amenities. This prestigious development features sleek, modern architecture that encompasses expansive apartments, seamlessly blending sophistication with the practicality of urban living.

Rising 13 stories above two subterranean levels, Milos offers 195 meticulously crafted residences, each designed to meet the discerning standards of families and professionals alike. Scheduled for completion in April 2027, this project promises timely delivery without compromising on quality.

Milos exemplifies an unwavering commitment to detail, showcasing interiors adorned with premium finishes and expansive porcelain tiling. Generous floor plans and floor-to-ceiling windows invite natural light, framing breathtaking views and enhancing the living experience. Soaring ceilings and thoughtfully conceived living spaces cultivate an atmosphere of understated luxury, transforming everyday life into an artful experience.
